## P2: Existential

Our communities are broken. We're bowling alone. Kids don't have rites of passages. They don't know where they fit in society so they fall into downward spirals of virtual life behind screens. They never fight the healthy battles to establish confidence in harder and more meaningful pursuits.

**Linked Goals:** G1, G2
**Board Advisor:** Anna Lembke (Psychology)

---

## P3: Ecological

Our supply chains are a house of cards propped up by fragile fossil fuel reserves. An unfathomable number of interconnected parts have to fit together perfectly for us to feed and house ourselves. Miraculously we haven't felt deeper disruptions. We take it all for granted and as a result are not prepared for the inevitable shocks that may be a few days, weeks, or even generations.

Nate Hagens calls this **The Great Simplification**.

**Linked Goals:** G2, G3
**Board Advisor:** Nate Hagens (Energy/Ecology)
