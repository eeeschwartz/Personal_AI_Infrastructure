# Mission

**Core life missions and purpose.**

---

## M1: Young Men & Purpose

Help young men develop purpose as we transition to a post-capitalism economic model based around the concepts of the Last Economy book by Emad Mostaque.

**Why:** P1 (Economic) and P2 (Existential) - young men are losing purpose as economic identity evaporates
**How:** Lead rites of passage retreats, teach them to fight and earn their rewards
**Timeline:** Ongoing, first retreat by July 2026

---

## M2: Community Resiliency

To bring people in Lexington together around community resiliency.

**Why:** P2 (Existential) and P3 (Ecological) - we need local networks before potential collapse
**How:** Newsletter, skill-building events, resource sharing
**Timeline:** Ongoing, 100 subscribers by March 2026
