## Personal Aphorisms

- **The magic you're looking for is in the work you're avoiding.**

- **When I am feeling low mood or low energy, 95% of the time it's because I have not:**
  1. Had deliberate cold exposure
  2. Gotten outside
  3. Worked out
  4. Gone for a walk
  5. Done anything else physical in the last 2 days

  Just go do that, and you'll instantly feel better.

## Context-Specific Wisdom

### For Hard Times
- The magic you're looking for is in the work you're avoiding.
- Just go outside and move your body.
- "We never give our bodies the signal that everything's OK now." — if stuck, the body may still be bracing.

### For Purpose
- Man's Search for Meaning framework: Purpose is the antidote.
- The King holds purpose at the center. When he leaves the throne, crises rush in to fill it.

### For Transformation
- Regulate first, rewire second, then take action. Never reverse this order.
- You cannot think your way out of a somatic pattern. The body must complete what it started.
- Backbone is not built — it is revealed by carving away what hides it.

### Daily Check-In (Wagner)
- "How free am I right now? How am I stuck?" — let the answer determine the practice.
