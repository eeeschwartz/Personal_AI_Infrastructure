## Personal Aphorisms

- **The magic you're looking for is in the work you're avoiding.**

- **When I am feeling low mood or low energy, 95% of the time it's because I have not:**
  1. Had deliberate cold exposure
  2. Gotten outside
  3. Worked out
  4. Gone for a walk
  5. Done anything else physical in the last 2 days

  Just go do that, and you'll instantly feel better.
